Developed a mobile application connecting music fans with bands, utilizing Android Studio, IntelliJ, and Spring Boot. Integrated Ticketmaster, Google Maps, and Spotify APIs. Utilized Maven for build automation and MySQL for database management. Managed code with Git and GitLab; documented with Swagger.
Riff Radar is a mobile application that revolutionizes the way music fans connect with their favorite bands. By providing a platform where users can discover live music events of all sizes, Riff Radar removes the barriers to concert hosting and attendance. The app allows attendees to search for concerts, purchase tickets, and get directions to venues. Bands can find and book venues, while venues can manage their listings and connect with potential performers. Additionally, the app enhances the user experience with features like music previews through Spotify, secure payment processing with Stripe, and social connections within a customized radius, creating a comprehensive ecosystem for live music discovery and event management.
